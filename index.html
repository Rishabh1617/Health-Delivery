Levothyroxine Synthroid

An artificial form of thyroxine (T4), a hormone that comes from the thyroid gland. T4 is low in people with hypothyroidism.Approves using levothyroxine for pituitary thyrotropin suppression in people with certain forms of thyroid cancer.

Atorvastatin Lipitor

It is the first-line treatment option for high cholesterol works by modifying amount of cholesterol the body makes in the liver. Atorvastatin blocks enzymes in the liver responsible for making cholesterol.

Metoprolol Lopressor

It is the class of blood pressure medications is beta-blockers. Metoprolol Lopressor is a beta-blocker that doctors use to treat angina,heart failure,heart attack,atrial fibrillation,flutter and high blood pressure.

Omeprazole Prilosec

A proton pump inhibitor lowers the amount of acid that the cells in the stomach lining produce.It is available as a prescription drug and as an over-the-counter medication at a lower dose.

Albuterol Ventolin

It is a drug available in a pressurized spray inhaler.It is the medication via oral inhalation.The drug reduces bronchospasm, which occurs when the muscles around the airways leading to the lungs.

Amlodipine Norvasc

It is the another class of blood pressure medications that doctors often prescribe is calcium channel blockers. These drugs help relax blood vessels and lower the pressure within them.

Metformin Gluophage

It is a diabetes medication.Prescribed for the first-line treatment option for people with a new diagnosis of the condition.This helps in lowering how much sugar the liver produces.

Simvastatin Zocor

It works in the same way as atorvastatin, and doctors prescribe it for the same reasons.In comparison with atorvastatin, doctors usually prescribe simvastatin for nighttime use. However, both drugs share drug interactions.
